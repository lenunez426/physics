\begin{minipage}{11.5cm}
\centering
\begin{itemize}
    \item[] \fillin[C][5mm]\ The ``pull'' that brings the object back toward equilibrium\\whenever it moves away.
    \item[] \fillin[B][5mm]\ The place where the object rests if undisturbed.
    \item[] \fillin[D][5mm]\ The time it takes for one complete cycle (back and forth).\\Measured in seconds (s).
    \item[] \fillin[F][5mm]\ The maximum distance moved from the equilibrium point.
    \item[] \fillin[A][5mm]\ Motion that repeats itself back and forth over the same path,\\centered around an equilibrium position.
    \item[] \fillin[E][5mm]\ How many cycles occur in one second. Measured in Hertz (Hz).
\end{itemize}
\end{minipage}
